Our Story

Vital Power Advanced Technologies

Established with a vision to revolutionize the energy sector in India, VPAT has rapidly grown into a trusted name for comprehensive solar solutions. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful idea: to make clean, renewable energy accessible and affordable for every home and business.

Based in Jadcherla, Telangana, we leverage cutting-edge technology and engineering expertise to design and deploy highly efficient solar photovoltaic systems. Our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction sets us apart in the industry.

Our Mission

To accelerate the adoption of solar energy by providing innovative, high-quality, and cost-effective solutions that deliver exceptional value and positive environmental impact.

Our Vision

To be India's premier solar energy company, known for pioneering sustainable technologies that power homes, industries, and a cleaner planet for future generations.
