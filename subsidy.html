Government Schemes

Solar Subsidy in India (PM Surya Ghar)

The Government of India is heavily promoting residential solar installations through subsidies. Under the PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana, homeowners can avail significant financial assistance to install rooftop solar panels.

  • Up to 3kW: High subsidy slab (₹30,000 per kW up to 2kW, ₹18,000 for 3rd kW)
  • Max Subsidy: ₹78,000 for systems 3kW and above.
  • Direct Transfer: Subsidy amount is credited directly to your bank account.
  • Eligibility: Must use domestically manufactured solar modules (DCR).

Note: At VPAT, we handle the entire subsidy application process on the National Portal for Rooftop Solar, ensuring a hassle-free experience for you.
